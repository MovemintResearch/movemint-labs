Peptide Blends

Glow Blend 70 mg

Contents per vial: GHK-Cu 50 mg, BPC-157 10 mg, TB-500 10 mg.

Research focus: models of tissue repair, collagen regulation, angiogenesis, and recovery pathways. GHK-Cu is widely studied for skin and hair research; BPC-157 and TB-500 appear in animal models involving tendon, ligament, and muscle repair.

In-vitro / pre-clinical only

KLOW Blend 80 mg

Custom peptide combination designed for exploratory work in wound healing, inflammation modulation, and recovery models.

Intended for lab teams needing a standardized vialized blend rather than mixing individual peptides for each experiment.

Custom research blend

Single Peptides

Tesamorelin 10 mg

Synthetic GHRH (growth hormone–releasing hormone) analogue used in research on growth hormone pulses, lipolysis, and body composition models. Frequently used to study GH/IGF-1 axis modulation.

GHRH analogue

Ipamorelin 10 mg

Selective growth hormone–releasing peptide (GHRP) studied for its ability to stimulate GH release with relatively low impact on prolactin and cortisol in animal and in-vitro work.

Selective GHRP

Semax 10 mg

Heptapeptide analogue researched for potential neuro-protective and neuro-modulatory properties in models involving stress response and cognitive endpoints.

Neuropeptide

Other Research Compounds

NAD⁺ 1000 mg

Oxidized form of nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, essential cofactor in redox reactions. Widely used in in-vitro work on mitochondrial function, sirtuin activation, and cellular energy metabolism.

Research chemical
