Research Summaries & Item Information

The following summaries organize each peptide or compound by major research area. Descriptions are based on how these agents have been explored in scientific and pre-clinical studies and are intended for laboratory and educational context only.

No claims of safety or efficacy are made. Nothing here is medical advice or dosing guidance for human use.

Metabolic & Body-Composition Research

5-Amino-1MQ

Small molecule that inhibits nicotinamide N-methyltransferase (NNMT), an enzyme involved in energy metabolism and methylation, especially in adipose tissue. Research models suggest that decreasing NNMT activity can influence fat-cell size and fat-mass dynamics.

Metabolic research

AOD9604

Modified fragment of human growth hormone (hGH 176–191) with a disulfide bridge. Initially developed as a lipolytic research compound, it has been studied in animal models of fat metabolism, heart disease, osteoarthritis/cartilage repair, and broader metabolic-syndrome endpoints.

Lipolysis / metabolic

Tesofensine

Serotonin–noradrenaline–dopamine reuptake inhibitor (SNDRI) studied in neurodegenerative and metabolic contexts. Research has examined appetite suppression, body-weight changes and energy expenditure, as well as potential applications in Parkinson’s and Alzheimer’s disease models.

Appetite / CNS metabolic

Semaglutide

GLP-1 receptor analogue investigated for glycemic control, insulin secretion, and cardiovascular, hepatic and pulmonary endpoints. Studies also examine its effects on appetite, delayed gastric emptying and weight regulation in pre-clinical and clinical models.

GLP-1 analogue

Tirzepatide

Synthetic dual GIP/GLP-1 agonist developed for type 2 diabetes and obesity research. Experimental work explores its impact on blood-glucose reduction, insulin sensitivity, satiety signalling and body-fat distribution.

GIP/GLP-1 dual agonist

Retatrutide

Tri-agonist targeting GLP-1, GIP and glucagon receptors. Research focuses on how combined signalling at these receptors affects basal metabolism, fat and glycogen utilization, and longer-term weight trajectories in metabolic models.

Triple incretin / glucagon

MOTS-c

Mitochondria-derived peptide examined for effects on metabolic homeostasis, exercise capacity, adiposity and insulin resistance. Studies investigate roles in skeletal, bone and energy-balance pathways linked to longevity models.

Mitochondrial metabolic peptide

Melanotan-1

Melanocortin analogue used in research involving skin-pigmentation pathways, UV response, cardiovascular markers, inflammation, cognition, stroke recovery and body-weight regulation via melanocortin receptor signalling.

Melanocortin / metabolic

Melanotan-2

Alpha-MSH analogue investigated for its effects on melanocortin receptors involved in pigmentation, appetite, reward and sexual behaviour. Models assess changes in melanin production, feeding, addictive behaviour and arousal.

Melanocortin / behaviour

Tissue Repair, Wound Healing & Anti-Fibrotic Research

BPC-157

Gastric-derived pentadecapeptide studied in models of muscle, tendon and ligament repair, as well as gastrointestinal integrity. Research has explored its effects in ulcer, “leaky gut” and inflammatory bowel models.

Tissue repair

Thymosin Beta-4

Peptide associated with actin modulation and tissue remodelling. Studies evaluate its role in new blood-vessel growth, wound healing, inflammation control and reduction of oxidative damage in cardiac and CNS models.

Regeneration / remodeling

B7-33

Peptide investigated for anti-fibrotic properties. Pre-clinical work suggests reductions in fibrosis in models of heart failure, lung injury and kidney damage, with interest in both acute and chronic fibrotic processes.

Anti-fibrotic

PEG-MGF

Pegylated mechano growth factor (MGF), a splice variant of IGF-1. Research explores its effects on lipid profiles, immune function and wound-healing dynamics, particularly in muscle or tissue stress models.

Growth-factor variant

KPV

Short peptide fragment studied for anti-inflammatory actions, including in inflammatory bowel and wound-healing models. Work has examined infection control, tissue repair and cosmetic outcome following injury.

Anti-inflammatory / repair

LL-37

Human cathelicidin-derived antimicrobial peptide. Research highlights antibacterial, antiviral and antifungal activity along with effects on inflammation, angiogenesis and potential anti-tumour activity.

Host defense peptide

ARA-290

Erythropoietin-derived peptide that signals through the innate repair receptor. Studies explore its impact on inflammatory regulation, neuropathic pain, wound repair, glycemic markers and lipid profiles.

Innate repair signalling

Thymalin

Thymulin-related peptide investigated for roles in inflammatory and pain pathways, immune modulation, cardiovascular protection, sleep regulation and longevity in various experimental models.

Thymic / repair

Oxytocin

Neuropeptide and hormone studied not only for social and behavioural circuits but also for wound-healing, cardiovascular markers and anxiety-related outcomes. Research models include social behaviour and autonomic regulation.

Neuropeptide / repair

Glow 70mg & Wolverin Blend 20mg

Custom blends combining peptides such as GHK-Cu, BPC-157 and TB-500, designed for exploratory work in tissue repair, collagen modulation, angiogenesis and recovery-oriented models where a standardized combination is desired.

Custom repair blends

GH Axis, Muscle & Anabolic Signaling

CJC-1295

Long-acting GHRH analogue evaluated for effects on pulsatile growth hormone and IGF-1. Used in research on muscle growth, tissue repair and metabolic control through GH-axis modulation.

GHRH analogue

Ipamorelin

Ghrelin-receptor agonist (GHS-R) classified as a selective GHRP. Research assesses its ability to increase GH and IGF-1 signalling with relatively focused receptor activity, in models of muscle, skeletal and connective-tissue support.

GHRP / ghrelin agonist

Tesamorelin

GHRH analogue studied in settings of abnormal fat distribution and metabolic dysfunction. Work includes GH/IGF-1 axis effects, visceral fat changes and potential cognitive endpoints.

GHRH analogue

Sermorelin

Short-acting GHRH analogue historically used to assess GH secretory capacity. Research has also explored influences on bone remodelling, scar structure and certain neurocognitive or seizure-related models.

GHRH analogue

GHRP-2

Growth hormone secretagogue that binds ghrelin/GHS receptors. Experimental data examine GH release, muscle growth, immune modulation and sleep-cycle architecture, including work on oral bioavailability.

GH secretagogue

Hexarelin

Synthetic GHRP analogue related to ghrelin. Beyond GH-axis research, it has been studied in cardiac-ischemia and heart-failure models, skeletal-muscle preservation and lipid-profile modulation.

GHRP / cardiometabolic

Follistatin-315

Naturally occurring activin and myostatin-binding protein. Research investigates its role in skeletal-muscle hypertrophy/hyperplasia, inflammatory signalling and reproductive-axis biology.

Myostatin / activin binding

Neurocognitive, Mood & CNS Research

Cerebrolysin

Peptide-based neurotrophic preparation studied in models of cognitive impairment, mood, neurodevelopmental conditions and neurotrauma. Research explores nerve repair, neurotoxicity mitigation and functional recovery.

Neurotrophic preparation

FGL

Peptide fragment derived from neural cell adhesion molecules. Experimental work examines neuroprotection, synaptic plasticity, memory, learning, mood regulation and neuroinflammation in models of brain injury and degeneration.

Neuroprotective peptide

Selank

Tuftsin-based peptide analogue investigated for anxiolytic, cognitive and pain-modulating effects. Studies look at memory, learning and stress-response pathways in CNS models.

Anxiety / cognition

Semax

ACTH-derived heptapeptide analogue with reported nootropic and neuroprotective properties in research. Work includes effects on BDNF, neurogenesis, immune markers and cardiovascular parameters.

ACTH-derived neuropeptide

Dihexa

Nootropic peptide derivative investigated for enhancement of cognitive function and synaptogenesis. It is studied in BDNF-related pathways and neurodegenerative models, including Alzheimer-type pathology.

BDNF-linked nootropic

PE-22-28

Spadin-derived peptide targeting TREK-1 potassium channels. Research explores fast-acting antidepressant-like effects, stroke recovery and neurodegenerative disease models.

TREK-1 modulator

P21

Nootropic peptide examined for its ability to influence BDNF levels, neurogenesis and the accumulation of amyloid and tau pathology in Alzheimer-type models.

Neurogenesis / Alzheimer models

DSIP

Delta sleep–inducing peptide studied in regulation of sleep, endocrine function, oxidative stress and cardiac contractility. Models also explore mood and depression-related endpoints.

Sleep / endocrine peptide

Mitochondrial, Longevity & Cellular Protection

NAD⁺

Central redox coenzyme (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) involved in energy metabolism and DNA repair. Research evaluates roles in muscle performance, neuronal resilience and age-related functional decline.

Redox cofactor

Epithalon

Synthetic derivative of epithalamin investigated as a telomerase activator. Studies examine telomere dynamics, circadian hormone rhythms (cortisol/melatonin) and broader longevity-related parameters.

Telomere / aging research

FOXO4-DRI

Peptide designed to interfere with FOXO4–p53 interactions. Research models explore senescent-cell clearance, organ function, insulin signalling and oxidative-stress responses in the context of aging biology.

Senolytic-oriented research

Humanin

Mitochondrial-encoded micro-peptide. Studies assess its ability to protect cells from apoptosis, support metabolic efficiency and influence age-related disease models through mitochondrial signalling.

Mitochondrial micro-peptide

SS31

Mitochondria-targeted peptide examined for effects on ATP production, mitochondrial membrane potential, inflammatory cytokines and resilience in neurodegenerative and metabolic disease models.

Mitochondrial-targeted peptide

Immune Modulation & Inflammation

Thymosin Alpha-1

Thymic peptide investigated as an immune modulator. Research explores antibacterial, antiviral and antifungal responses, immune-cell activation, tumour-immunity models, wound healing and vaccine-response augmentation.

Immune modulation

Vilon

Peptide with immunomodulatory and anti-aging interest. Studies examine its effects on immune regulation, tissue repair and tumour-related models.

Immunomodulatory peptide

VIP (Vasoactive Intestinal Peptide)

Neuropeptide examined for anti-inflammatory, anti-fibrotic and neuroprotective actions. Research includes immune-regulation, CNS protection and fibrotic disease models.

Neuropeptide / immune

Reproductive, Sexual & Hormonal Axis

Kisspeptin-10

Peptide that stimulates GnRH and LH release. Research focuses on reproductive hormone regulation, testosterone dynamics and potential reversal of certain age-related changes in hypothalamic–pituitary–gonadal signalling.

Reproductive-axis peptide

PT-141

Melanocortin-receptor agonist studied in models of sexual arousal and haemodynamic regulation. Experimental work also explores immune activation and haemorrhage-related applications.

Melanocortin / sexual function

Oncologic & Cell-Targeting Research

PNC-27

Peptide designed to bind HDM-2 and modulate p53 activity. Research examines selective effects on cancer-cell membranes and tumour-cell viability in pre-clinical oncologic models.

Oncology-focused peptide
