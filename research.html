Peptide Blends

Glow Blend 70 mg

Contents per vial: GHK-Cu 50 mg, BPC-157 10 mg, TB-500 10 mg.

Research focus: models involving tissue repair, collagen modulation, angiogenesis, and recovery. GHK-Cu is widely used in skin and hair research; BPC-157 and TB-500 commonly appear in pre-clinical work on tendon, ligament, and skeletal-muscle recovery.

In-vitro / pre-clinical only

KLOW Blend 80 mg

Custom peptide combination formulated for exploratory work in wound healing, inflammation modulation, and recovery-oriented models. A standardized vialized blend can be useful where repeated mixing of individual peptides would add variability between experiments.

Custom research blend

Single Peptides

Tesamorelin 10 mg

Synthetic analogue of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). Frequently used to study GH pulse dynamics, IGF-1 signalling, lipolysis, and body-composition endpoints in pre-clinical models.

GHRH analogue

Ipamorelin 10 mg

Selective growth hormone-releasing peptide (GHRP) investigated for its ability to stimulate GH release with relatively modest effects on prolactin and cortisol in animal and in-vitro work.

Selective GHRP

Semax 10 mg

Heptapeptide analogue researched for potential neuro-modulatory and neuro-protective roles in models involving stress response, learning behaviour, and cognitive performance endpoints.

Neuropeptide

BPC-157 10 mg

Pentadecapeptide originally derived from a gastric protein fragment. Appears in many pre-clinical studies exploring tendon and ligament repair, gut integrity, angiogenesis, and inflammatory modulation in animal models.

Single peptide

MOT-C 10 mg

Mitochondria-derived peptide investigated in pre-clinical models of metabolism, insulin sensitivity, and cellular response to energetic stress. Often used to explore mitochondrial signalling and glucose handling pathways.

Experimental peptide

Incretin-Pathway Research (Tirzepatide)

Tirzepatide 15 mg

Class: dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist analogue used in metabolic research. The 15 mg vial format is suited to smaller scale or pilot studies on glucose control and body-weight endpoints in pre-clinical models.

Peptide – research only

Tirzepatide 30 mg

Same research compound in a higher-content vial, useful where multiple arms, dose-response curves, or extended-duration animal experiments are being run from a single batch.

Peptide – research only

Tirzepatide 60 mg

High-content presentation for labs that require larger cumulative amounts from one lot, such as long-term metabolic studies or combined protocol designs. Composition is equivalent to lower-content vials; only the total amount per vial differs.

Peptide – research only

Other Research Compounds

NAD⁺ 1000 mg

Oxidized nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, a central redox cofactor in cellular metabolism. Widely used in cell-based assays exploring mitochondrial function, oxidative stress, sirtuin activity, and energy-balance pathways.
