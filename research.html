Peptide Blends

Glow Blend 70 mg

Contents per vial: GHK-Cu 50 mg, BPC-157 10 mg, TB-500 10 mg.

Research focus: models of tissue repair, collagen modulation, angiogenesis, and recovery pathways. GHK-Cu is widely studied in skin and hair research, while BPC-157 and TB-500 commonly appear in pre-clinical models involving tendon, ligament, and muscle.

In-vitro / pre-clinical only

KLOW Blend 80 mg

Custom peptide combination formulated for exploratory work in wound healing, inflammation modulation, and recovery-oriented models where a standardized vialized blend is preferred over mixing single peptides each time.

Custom research blend

Single Peptides

Tesamorelin 10 mg

Synthetic analogue of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). Used in research examining GH pulse dynamics, IGF-1 signalling, lipolysis, and body-composition endpoints in pre-clinical models.

GHRH analogue

Ipamorelin 10 mg

Selective growth hormone-releasing peptide (GHRP) investigated for its ability to stimulate GH release with relatively modest interaction with prolactin and cortisol in animal and in-vitro work.

Selective GHRP

Semax 10 mg

Heptapeptide analogue researched for potential neuro-modulatory and neuro-protective effects in models involving stress response, learning behaviour, and cognitive endpoints.

Neuropeptide
